Strategic Protocols: How to Play

CyberTic Evolution follows standard Tic Tac Toe rules but scales the AI difficulty across 100 levels.

  • The Objective: Align three symbols (X) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the AI (O) does.
  • Progressive Difficulty: As you clear levels, the AI's "Neural Logic" becomes more precise.
  • Impossible Tiers: From level 90 onwards, the AI uses the Minimax Algorithm. In these levels, a draw is the best possible outcome for a human player.

Winning Strategies:

Starting in the center offers the most defensive flexibility, while starting in a corner sets up the highest probability for a "Fork" (two winning paths at once).
